Pangdonglai: Customer Value-Driven Supply Chain

Research Team Member · Nov 2024 – Dec 2024

Analysed Pangdonglai’s differentiated supply-chain strategy, connecting assortment, pricing, and service design to customer loyalty and scalability.

Key Contributions

  • Summarised operational metrics, market share, and industry outlook using public filings and authorised databases.
  • Deconstructed Pangdonglai’s business model to pinpoint competitive strengths in supply chain orchestration.
  • Analysed how signature practices influence product mix, pricing power, and customer relationship management.
  • Evaluated the replicability of Pangdonglai’s model for wider retail contexts.

Toolkit & Methods

  • Desk research
  • Financial benchmarking
  • Customer value analysis
  • Retail strategy

Outcomes

  • Documented a playbook of supply-chain practices that sustain Pangdonglai’s customer loyalty and margin profile.
  • Outlined conditions for adapting the model to other retailers exploring value-driven supply chains.
