Yanghe Distillery Financial Statement Analysis (2019–2023)

Team Analyst · 2019–2023 · Financial statement analysis & peer benchmarking

Conducted an end-to-end financial statement analysis of Yanghe (2019–2023), benchmarking against key peers. The work covers industry context, balance sheet quality, working-capital dynamics, and relative valuation (PE/PB), translating findings into an investment-style conclusion and risk checklist.

Key Contributions

  • Built a 2019–2023 three-statement analysis framework and benchmarked Yanghe against major peers to pinpoint structural strengths/pressures.
  • Interpreted industry context and cycle positioning to frame Yanghe’s growth and competitive dynamics.
  • Assessed balance-sheet quality through asset structure, liquidity signals, and capital allocation patterns.
  • Diagnosed working-capital dynamics (contract liabilities / channel cash reservoir) and discussed sustainability under channel normalization.
  • Produced relative valuation using PE/PB peer set and summarized key upside vs. execution risks in a structured conclusion.
