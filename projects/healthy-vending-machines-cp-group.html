Healthy Vending Machines – CP Group

Survey Lead (AHP & Sampling) · Nov 2023 – Mar 2024 · Shanghai pilot market research

For CP Group’s “healthy vending machine” initiative in Shanghai, I led the AHP-based location-selection logic and the end-to-end survey methodology. I designed the questionnaire structure, calculated required sample size, validated measurement reliability/validity, and implemented an AHP-weighted stratified sampling plan to prioritize high-potential districts and streets—translating analysis into actionable site-selection recommendations.

Key Contributions

  • Designed the survey and questionnaire logic for location selection, product/price preferences, and usage scenarios, ensuring operationally testable metrics.
  • Calculated required sample size under 95% confidence and a 3% margin of error, and planned distribution with an assumed ~90% validity rate; executed field + online collection with 443 valid responses (from 500 issued).
  • Ran measurement quality checks including item discrimination (t-test), reliability (Cronbach’s α), and validity (KMO/Bartlett), confirming the scale is suitable for downstream modeling.
  • Built an AHP hierarchy and pairwise comparison framework to weight macro location factors (population, economy, transport, risk, competition) and verified consistency (CR < 0.1).
  • Implemented AHP-weighted stratified sampling: prioritized target districts (e.g., Minhang/Jing’an/Putuo) and allocated quotas by population weight; combined stratified sampling with simple random sampling at community level.
  • Produced site-selection recommendations and a short “how-to” playbook linking AHP results to real-world rollout strategy.
