Digital Business Environment & Corporate Innovation

Research project · 2018–2023 A-share panel · Smart Courts index

This ongoing research examines how smart court construction shapes firms’ innovation outcomes through the rule-of-law business environment. I led the end-to-end workflow: CSMAR panel assembly, city-level Smart Court index mapping, tax-uncertainty index construction, and robustness / cross-sectional analyses. As the paper is under review, this page presents selected visuals only.

Key Contributions

  • Built and cleaned a firm-year panel dataset from CSMAR and related city-level governance indicators (2018–2023).
  • Constructed a Smart Court index at the city level and mapped it to firm locations to enable econometric testing.
  • Designed the tax-uncertainty index (multi-dimension aggregation) and integrated it for heterogeneity analysis.
  • Implemented a robustness toolbox (variable substitution, matching/selection correction, IV-style reasoning where applicable) to stress-test findings.
  • Conducted cross-sectional analyses by regional development, shock periods, tax uncertainty, and digital transformation proxies.
  • Produced a poster-ready narrative and a full research mindmap to communicate identification logic and mechanisms.
