Healthy Vending Machines · CP Group

Team Lead · Apr 2024 – Jun 2024

Delivered a data-backed go-to-market playbook for healthy vending machines in Shanghai by triangulating consumer surveys, machine-learning models, and merchandising analytics.

What We Built

  • Used Analytic Hierarchy Process to rank Shanghai business districts across five macro criteria, prioritising Hongqiao, Caojiadu, and Wuning New City.
  • Executed PPS sampling and a 443-respondent survey aligned to the 4P marketing mix to profile demand.
  • Scored 30+ SKUs with Random Forest, optimised planograms via exploratory factor analysis, and ranked 11 promotional tactics with XGBoost.

Toolkit & Methods

  • Analytic Hierarchy Process
  • PPS sampling
  • Random Forest
  • Exploratory factor analysis
  • XGBoost
  • 4P survey design

Results

  • Prioritised launch zones and SKU assortments that balanced health positioning with revenue potential.
  • Recommended high-impact promotion bundles (timed discounts, membership perks, IP co-brands, charity tie-ins).
