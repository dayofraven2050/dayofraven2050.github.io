Beyond the Skin: How Human and Non-Human Avatars Shape In-Game Purchase Intentions

Project Lead (End-to-end implementation) · 14-day online experiment · Alibaba Cloud deployment

An end-to-end consumer-behavior web experiment that simulates in-game avatar and virtual goods choices. I owned the experiment build, deployment, instrumentation, and analysis pipeline—delivering clean, export-ready behavioral logs and survey measures for ANOVA/SEM/PROCESS modeling.

Key Contributions

  • Built and deployed a full web experiment (HTML/JS + JSON stimuli) and ran it for 14 days on Alibaba Cloud, simulating a real gameplay flow.
  • Implemented client-side analytics (session clicks, dwell time via IntersectionObserver, checkout attempts) with an admin-only CSV export, enabling backend-free, analysis-ready datasets.
  • Owned the research workflow: literature scan, experimental procedure planning, and methodology design (three-way ANOVA, SEM, SPSS PROCESS).
  • Conducted data processing and modeling across multiple studies/conditions; delivered comparative analysis outputs and cleaned exports for subsequent reporting.
