Elden Ring in the Marketplace

Independent Analyst · Nov 2024 – Feb 2025

Uncovered player archetypes and market dynamics for Elden Ring by blending multi-source data—search trends, community sentiment, and survey analytics—into a single strategic narrative.

Research Narrative

  • Scraped a full year of Baidu Index data for Elden Ring and competitor titles; used NVivo to code six core demand buckets.
  • Fused 778 valid questionnaires with search signals, employing an RFM model to carve eight distinct player value archetypes.
  • Ran Difference-in-Differences to show that the 20 Aug 2024 launch of Black Myth: Wukong lifted combined searches for Elden Ring & Sekiro by +3,387 index points.
  • Processed 20k Steam and Reddit posts using jieba tokenisation, Baidu Cloud sentiment API, and LDA clustering to surface four DLC pain points for targeted messaging.

Toolkit & Stack

  • Python web scraping
  • NVivo thematic coding
  • RFM segmentation
  • Difference-in-Differences
  • Sentiment analysis (Baidu API)
  • LDA topic modeling

Impact

  • Delivered persona-based retention and DLC communication strategies for gaming marketing teams.
  • Validated cross-IP lift windows to inform launch partnership timing and content bundling.
