Elden Ring in the Marketplace: Consumer Persona Typologies & Market Dynamics Analysis

Independent Analyst · Nov 2024 – Feb 2025 · Search trends + survey + UGC analytics

An end-to-end marketplace insight project that links search demand, survey-based segmentation, and UGC mining to explain Elden Ring’s consumer personas, competitive shocks, and DLC pain points—translating analytics into actionable messaging and patch-note priorities.

Key Contributions

  • Scraped a full year of Baidu Index data on Elden Ring and key rivals; used NVivo to structure player needs into six demand buckets.
  • Merged search insights with 778 valid questionnaires and applied an RFM model to segment players into eight value archetypes.
  • Applied Difference-in-Differences to show the 20 Aug 2024 launch of Black Myth: Wukong spiked combined searches for Elden Ring & Sekiro by +3,387 index points, evidencing a cross-title lift window.
  • Scraped ~20k Steam/Reddit posts; ran jieba tokenization + sentiment scoring (Baidu Cloud API) and LDA topic modeling to surface four DLC pain-point themes for targeted messaging.
