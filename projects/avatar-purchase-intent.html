Beyond the Skin: Avatar Design & Purchase Intent

Team Lead · Oct 2024 – Jan 2025

Designed a cloud-hosted experimental environment to test how human versus non-human avatars influence in-game purchase intentions, marrying behavioural telemetry with advanced statistical modeling.

Key Contributions

  • Coded an HTML/JavaScript front end with JSON-driven stimuli and deployed it to Alibaba Cloud for a 14-day simulated gameplay run.
  • Instrumented a custom dashboard capturing session-level clicks, dwell time, and checkout attempts for seamless export to SPSS & AMOS.
  • Led literature review, experimental design, and diagnostics for five sequential studies, applying three-way ANOVA, SEM, and SPSS PROCESS.
  • Coordinated the research team, ensuring protocol adherence, data quality, and timely delivery of analyses.

Toolkit & Methods

  • HTML/JS front-end engineering
  • Alibaba Cloud deployment
  • Three-way ANOVA
  • Structural Equation Modeling
  • SPSS PROCESS

Insights

  • Quantified the interaction between avatar anthropomorphism, narrative framing, and purchase intent.
  • Delivered design guidelines for avatar customization and monetisation experiments.
